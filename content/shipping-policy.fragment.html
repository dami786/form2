IS SHIPPING FREE?
Shipping is a flat 9% on all team orders. For individual orders, shipping will vary based on location.
WHY IS MY ORDER BEING SHIPPED IN DIFFERENT PACKAGES?
If you have a multi-item order, we may ship it in separate packages depending on which item is available the fastest. Alternatively, if an item is popular and on a bit of a backorder, we might ship your items at different times, in different packages, to prevent holding up your order and to get it to you as quick as possible!
WHEN WILL I RECEIVE MY ORDER?
Orders are shipped out directly from any of our many domestic & international warehouses and they will do everything they can to delivery your order as fast as they can. Due to the popularity of our offers, please allow an estimated 10-20 business days weeks for your order to arrive to the USA (varies from product to product). Other countries can take an estimated 10-30 business days (varies from product to product) due to distance traveling and customs. Please note, that due to the extreme popularity of our products, these are only estimates.
WHAT HAPPENS IF MY ORDER GETS STUCK OR LOST IN THE MAIL?
If an order gets stuck at customs, sent back, or even lost during the delivery process, we apologize! The postal service is out of our control, but we will do everything we can to make sure you receive your package. To avoid these issues, please make sure that you have the correct address submitted when you place your order. If you fail to put a correct address with your order, we are not responsible for covering the cost of a new item.
If your item or package is stolen, file a report with your local post office as soon as possible. We are not responsible for stolen packages, once delivery is confirmed by the carrier, it is your responsibility to ensure the safety of your package.

WILL I BE CHARGED FOR CUSTOMS AND TAXES?
We automatically charge customs and taxes (if applicable) at checkout. This is to ensure a smooth shipment that will not be held up upon reaching your country's customs office.
